ROLEX SUBMARINER-(110,000USD)
The Rolex Submariner is an iconic timepiece renowned for its robust design and exceptional functionality. Originally launched in 1953, this watch has become synonymous with diving excellence and luxury.
Key Features: - Water Resistance: Up to 300 meters (1,000 feet), making it perfect for diving enthusiasts.
- Rotatable Bezel: The unidirectional rotating bezel allows divers to track immersion time accurately.
- Classic Dial: Available in black or green, the luminous hour markers and hands ensure readability in low-light conditions.
-Durable Construction: Crafted from Oystersteel, known for its resistance to corrosion, the Submariner boasts a scratch-resistant sapphire crystal and a sturdy case.
- Automatic Movement: Powered by Rolex's in-house movements, offering precision and reliability with a power reserve of approximately 48 hours.
The Rolex Submariner embodies the perfect blend of style and performance, making it an essential addition to any luxury watch collection. Whether you're exploring the depths of the ocean or attending a formal event, the Submariner is a statement piece that never goes out of style.




ROLEX DATE JUST-(41,000USD)
The Rolex Datejust is a classic and iconic wristwatch known for its timeless design and versatile elegance. Launched in 1945, it was the first self-winding waterproof chronometer wristwatch to display the date through a window at 3 o'clock, magnified by a Cyclops lens on the crystal. Key features include: - Case: Typically available in sizes ranging from 31mm to 41mm, with options in stainless steel, gold (yellow, white, or rose), and Rolesor (a combination of gold and steel).
- Dial* Available in various colors and finishes, often with index or Roman numeral hour markers.
- Bracelet: Commonly found on the Oyster bracelet, featuring a combination of brushed and polished links, though other options like the Jubilee bracelet are also popular.
- Movement: Powered by Rolex's in-house automatic movements, known for their precision and reliability.
- Water Resistance: Generally water-resistant to 100 meters (330 feet), making it suitable for everyday wear.
The Datejust's combination of sophistication and durability makes it a favored choice for many watch enthusiasts and collectors. Its design is versatile enough to complement both formal and casual attire, solidifying its status as a staple in luxury timepieces.

5. Status Symbol:

6. Customization:

The Rolex Day-Date stands out not only for its luxurious appearance but also for its robust features, making it a quintessential timepiece for those who appreciate both elegance and functionality.




ROLEX COSMOGRAPH DYTONA-(29,000USD)
The Rolex Cosmograph Daytona is an iconic chronograph watch renowned for its precision, sporty design, and rich motorsport heritage. Here’s an overview highlighting its key features:
1. Sporty Elegance:
- Case Material: Typically crafted from stainless steel, gold, or platinum, combining durability with luxury.
- Size: Standard case diameter of 40mm, making it suitable for both casual and formal occasions.
2. Chronograph Functionality:
- Sub-Dials: Features three sub-dials for measuring elapsed time, including a 30-minute counter, a 12-hour counter, and a running seconds hand.
- Tachymetric Scale: Bezel often includes a tachymetric scale, allowing for speed calculations based on time and distance.
3. High-Performance Movement:
- Caliber: Equipped with Rolex's in-house chronograph movement, ensuring accuracy and reliability.
- COSC Certification: Chronometer-certified for precision, maintaining exceptional timekeeping standards.
4. Distinctive Dial:
- Color Variations: Available in various dial colors, including black, white, and champagne, often with contrasting sub-dials for enhanced readability.
- Luminous Markers: Equipped with luminescent hour markers and hands for visibility in low light.
- Oyster Bracelet: Usually features the robust Oyster bracelet with an Easylink extension system for added comfort and adjustability.
5. Motorsport Heritage:
- Connection to Racing: Designed in collaboration with motorsport enthusiasts, named after the famous Daytona International Speedway.
- Cultural Icon: Favored by collectors and motorsport fans alike, celebrated in popular culture, especially by actors like Paul Newman.
6. Bracelet Options:
The Rolex Cosmograph Daytona is not just a watch; it’s a statement piece that embodies luxury, performance, and a passion for motorsport. Its combination of technical excellence and stylish design makes it a coveted choice among watch aficionados.




ROLEX OYSTER PERPETUAL-(72,000USD)
The Rolex Oyster Perpetual is a timeless symbol of luxury and precision. Known for its impeccable craftsmanship and iconic design, this watch seamlessly blends functionality with elegance.
Key Features:
Case:
Made from high-quality stainless steel (or optional gold/platinum). The classic Oyster case is designed for durability and water resistance (up to 100 meters).
Dial:
Available in a variety of colors, including black, white, blue, and champagne. Luminous hour markers and hands for excellent readability in low light. Minimalistic design with no bezel, enhancing its sleek appearance.
Movement:
Powered by Rolex's self-winding Caliber 3230 (or equivalent), ensuring precision and reliability. Features a Parachrom hairspring for enhanced resistance to magnetic fields.
Bracelet:
Equipped with a comfortable and robust Oyster bracelet. Made from the same high-quality material as the case, with an Oysterlock clasp for secure fastening.
Dimensions:
Available in various sizes, typically 36mm to 41mm in diameter, catering to different wrist sizes.
Special Features;
Water Resistance: The Oyster case provides exceptional water resistance, making it ideal for everyday wear and adventures.
Self-Winding:
The Perpetual rotor allows the watch to wind itself with the natural motion of your wrist. Versatility: Its understated elegance makes it suitable for both formal occasions and casual wear.
